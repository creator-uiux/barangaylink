Your Direct Link to Local Updates and Services

This project is a PHP-based web application that allows residents to view announcements, upcoming events, ongoing and future projects, and submit inquiries, concerns, and requests for documents to be addressed promptly. It addresses the problem of delayed responses and unnecessary visits. The primary users are barangay residents and officials.

Our Mission

To empower residents and barangay officials by providing a modern digital platform that delivers timely updates, transparent services, and simplified transactions. BarangayLink exists to reduce delays, improve communication, and strengthen trust between the community and local governance.

Our Vision

By 2028, BarangayLink aims to become the benchmark for digital barangay governance, where every resident can access information instantly, request services seamlessly, and feel actively connected to their community.

Core Values

Transparency

We promote open and honest communication by ensuring that all announcements, events, and projects are accessible to every resident.

Accessibility

We design the platform to be inclusive and mobile-friendly, making it easy for residents of all ages to use.

Efficiency

We streamline barangay processes to save time, reduce manual errors, and deliver faster responses.

Community Empowerment

We strengthen citizen involvement by giving residents a direct voice in governance through inquiries, requests, and feedback.
